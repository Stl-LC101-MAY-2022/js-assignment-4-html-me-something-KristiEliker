Dragon Age Inquisition

I love Dragon Age Inquisition for its world-building, emotion, and excellent open world gameplay. It takes me away to another world every time I play it. I adore the characters, and trying out every combination of classes and races. Adventuring across Thedas never gets tiring, nor does going through the epic story of the Inquisitor!

Total War: Warhammer 3

Total War: Warhammer 3 is one of my favorite games because it presents fantasy battles on a scale unheard of, with a host of memorable and awesome characters built on decades of lore. I enjoy the empire building aspects, growing settlements, and conquering terroritories as I spread my forces across the Old World. There there's the real time battles, where thousands of soldiers clash in epic confrontations. Dragons, giants, and many more terrifying beasts fight against brave soldiers and powerful wizards on expansive battlefields. There's nothing else like it.
